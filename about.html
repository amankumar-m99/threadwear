Its all started with...

Its all started with the idea to make profit by giving profit. Threadwear is a fresh startup aimed to provide the quality clothing for men, women and kids at a relatively cheaper price.It is a huge online-store of trending and stylish clothing products that are availed to you at your doorstep. We have an enormous stock of readymade clothing products to be sold to our customers via online mode.

How we do it ?

We directly connect the sellers/manufacturers to our customers, eliminating the role of any intermediate body. We forward your order details to the concerned seller/manufacturer to prepare, pack and ship. Our delivery partners take the product from the seller and deliver your product directly at the address provided by you at the time of placing the order.

Using our services

You can take benefit of our services in just few simple steps. You can visit and browse our website and find your perfect piece. Once you are certain to buy the particular product, add it to your cart and proceed to checkout.

Regarding your money

Your money is absolutely safe and yours and only yours untill you recieve your order-delivery. We shall not insist you to pay us any amount of money prior to your order-delivery. You can pay safely at the time of delivery. However, we always welcome advance payments from you regarding any of your order.
